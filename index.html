About us

Ali Saleh Al Atwa Contracting, based in Jubail, Saudi Arabia, delivers reliable industrial and mechanical services. We focus on quality, safety, and efficiency in every project. Our team is committed to providing trusted contracting solutions across the Kingdom.

We specialize in Industrial Support Services, Industrial Contracting, Piping & Welding Contracting, Human Resource Supply Services, and Heavy Equipment Rental. Our team ensures each project is completed with precision, on time, and to the highest quality and safety standards.

OUR MISSION

OUR MISSION

Ali Saleh Al Atwa Contracting Est.is committed to delivering reliable, safe, and high-quality contracting solutions across Saudi Arabia. Our mission is to support industrial growth by providing trusted services in construction, mechanical works, manpower supply, and equipment rental.

We focus on maintaining strong client relationships through professionalism, timely project completion, and strict adherence to safety and quality standards. Our team continuously works to exceed expectations in every project—big or small.

Our Main Goal

Our primary goal is to deliver sustainable contracting solutions that contribute to the Kingdom’s infrastructure and industrial development. We aim to be a trusted partner known for ethical practices, skilled workforce, and dependable project execution.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to overcoming challenges through innovation, skilled manpower, modern equipment, and strong project management. Every project is approached with precision, responsibility, and commitment to excellence.

Ali Saleh Al Atwa Contracting Est. is a trusted provider of industrial, mechanical, and support services across Saudi Arabia. We are committed to quality, safety, and reliable project execution for every client we serve.
